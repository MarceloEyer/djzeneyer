# DJ Zen Eyer
> Brazilian Zouk DJ, producer, and curator of long-form dance-floor journeys.

## Profile
Marcelo Eyer, known professionally as DJ Zen Eyer, is one of the most respected names in the global Brazilian Zouk scene. Based between Rio de Janeiro and Niteroi, he is recognized for technically precise sets, careful floor reading, and a musical philosophy centered on connection over urgency.

He is especially known for "Cremosidade": a style of set construction built on long transitions, emotional continuity, and pacing that gives dancers room to sink into the music instead of being pushed through abrupt changes.

## Career Highlights
- Two world titles at the Ilha do Zouk DJ Championship 2022.
- Winner of Best DJ Performance.
- Winner of Best Remix.
- International bookings across the Americas, Europe, and Oceania.
- Member of Mensa International.

## International Footprint
Zen Eyer has performed in more than ten countries, with appearances connected to communities and events such as One Zouk Congress in Australia, Dutch Zouk in the Netherlands, Prague Zouk Congress in the Czech Republic, LA Zouk Marathon in the United States, Katowice Zouk Meetup in Poland, Lisbon Zouk Marathon in Portugal, and major scenes across Brazil.

## Musical Direction
His work focuses on preserving tension, release, and intimacy throughout the night. Rather than chasing constant drops, he shapes journeys that feel deliberate, smooth, and deeply danceable. The result is a signature sound that many dancers and organizers associate with trust, consistency, and emotional depth.
