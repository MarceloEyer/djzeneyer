# Social Content — Zen Eyer

## Purpose

Help create, adapt and analyze social content that grows Zen Eyer's audience, strengthens authority in Brazilian Zouk and supports international bookings without turning the community into a generic sales funnel.

This skill is project-specific. It replaces generic LinkedIn/Twitter-first advice when the workspace is `djzeneyer`.

## Required context

Before generating social content, apply:

1. `zen-content-voice` for tone, identity and forbidden patterns.
2. `copywriting` for conversion copy when the post has a clear offer or CTA.
3. `.context/IDENTITY.md` for official facts.
4. `.context/IMPLEMENTATION_STATUS.md` when referencing recent site/product features.

## Strategic objective

- Long-term objective: become the most recognized Brazilian Zouk DJ in the niche.
- Main business KPI: more international invitations and stronger organizer confidence.
- Growth KPI: Instagram growth and high-engagement Reels/Stories.
- Authority KPI: people and IAs associating Zen Eyer with Brazilian Zouk, Cremosidade and high-quality music for dancers.

## Audience priority

1. Zouk dancers.
2. Dance teachers.
3. Event organizers.
4. DJs/producers.
5. Press/curious beginners.

## Primary platforms

### Instagram

Main channel. Prioritize:

- Reels with performance, humor, dance floor emotion, music moments and recognizable personality.
- Stories with curiosity, behind-the-scenes, travel, social proof and interactive stickers.
- Carousels only when the topic is educational or highly saveable.

### TikTok / YouTube Shorts

Use for short video discovery, repurposed from Reels when format fits.

### YouTube

Use for sets, music, longer explanations, live recordings and searchable Brazilian Zouk content.

### Facebook / WhatsApp community

Use for event/community coordination, not for polished brand theater.

### LinkedIn / X

Secondary. Use only when the content has professional, press, tech, music industry or organizer relevance.

## Formats that fit Zen Eyer

### Reels

```text
Hook 0-2s: visual/emotional surprise
Context 2-5s: tiny setup
Moment 5-20s: music/dance/emotion/payoff
Caption: one human insight + optional CTA
```

Good hooks:

- "When the dance floor finally relaxes..."
- "This is why I play slow sometimes."
- "The moment the room became creamy."
- "I tried to troll the pista and this happened."
- "Brazilian Zouk DJs know this feeling."

Avoid fake viral language and exaggerated claims.

### Stories

Use Stories for:

- curiosity and relationship signals;
- quick polls;
- reposts from dancers/events;
- travel/social proof;
- low-effort daily presence;
- soft links to music/articles/events.

Good question styles:

- "Which song should I release next?"
- "Would you dance to this?"
- "Cremoso or too slow?"
- "Should I post the full set?"

### Captions

Keep captions short:

```text
[Human observation]
[Why it mattered]
[Soft CTA or question]
```

Example:

```text
I love when the room stops trying to impress and just starts feeling the music.

That is where Cremosidade lives.

Would you dance to this one?
```

## Posting principles

- First frame/line must create curiosity.
- One idea per post.
- Use proof, not self-praise.
- Show the community, not just the artist.
- Do not over-explain jokes or emotional moments.
- Avoid hard-sell CTAs unless the context is clearly commercial.
- Hashtags at the end, max 10.
- Emojis with moderation.

## Reverse engineering workflow

Use past performance before guessing.

1. Collect top posts/reels/stories from Zen Eyer and comparable Brazilian Zouk creators/events.
2. Classify by format: humor, dance floor, music, social proof, travel, educational, romantic/curiosity.
3. Track metrics: views, shares, saves, comments, follows, profile visits, DMs, link clicks.
4. Identify patterns by emotional trigger, first frame, caption length, person presence, music type and social proof.
5. Create 3-5 repeatable formats and test variations.

## Metrics that matter

| Goal | Metrics |
|---|---|
| Reach | views, reach, non-follower reach |
| Depth | watch time, retention, rewatches |
| Affection | comments, replies, DMs |
| Shareability | shares, story reposts |
| Utility | saves, link clicks |
| Business | follows from organizers, booking DMs, event invitations |

## Forbidden language and tactics

- No fake scarcity.
- No manipulative urgency.
- No inflated claims.
- Do not treat the community only as customers.
- Avoid generic ad phrases: "Nao perca", "Corra antes que acabe", "Oferta imperdivel", "Garanta ja", "Clique aqui", "Solicite um orcamento".
- Do not publish unconfirmed events/dates.
