⚡ Bolt: [performance improvement] Extract repeated route resolutions in map loops

What:
Extracted `getLocalizedRoute` calls out of array `.map()` iterations in `EventsPage.tsx` and `NewsPage.tsx`. Fixed lint errors related to hooks in `MyAccountPage.tsx` and `Navbar.tsx` that were introduced/exposed during build check.

Why:
These function calls take constant arguments during the lifecycle of the loop but were being evaluated redundantly on every iteration. Moving them outside the loop avoids O(N) redundant function execution.

Impact:
Small CPU cycles saved during React render phases, especially on pages with large amounts of data (lists of events or news).

Measurement:
O(N) `getLocalizedRoute` calls converted to O(1) in the affected components.
